                              ISSUE RESOLVED
================================================================================

Problem:
  Manual items added to billing form were not persisting to database,
  not appearing in Order Management list, and not showing in PDF invoices.

Root Cause:
  Frontend edit modal was extracting manual items from wrong field.
  - Backend stores in: manual_items field
  - Frontend was looking in: items array with is_manual flag
  - Field names didn't match: item_name vs name

Solution:
  Updated frontend to use manual_items field directly with correct field names.

Impact:
  Single line change fixes entire manual items persistence flow.
